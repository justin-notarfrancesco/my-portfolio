## Overview

A long-form data-viz piece exploring how the city moves. Ridership figures animate along
stylized subway-line diagrams as the reader scrolls, blending editorial and interactive.

## What I built

- A scroll-driven narrative engine syncing prose, canvas animation, and D3 scales.
- A performant hybrid renderer: SVG for crisp line diagrams, canvas for dense point clouds.
- Reduced-motion and keyboard-navigable fallbacks for the full experience.

## Outcome

Featured in two frontend newsletters; ~30k reads in its first month.
